Tron Patrol

The first game I made using the Phaser3 framework. The gameplay of this game is inspired from another game called rocket patrol. The twist is that instead of crashing into the ships you instead destroy the lightcycles with your trail. (Figuring out how to make that trail work was a pain btw!!) I also made the art and music all on my own which was a nice change of pace from always having to do programming. Check it out on itch.io!
